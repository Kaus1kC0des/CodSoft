Welcome to the Spam Classifier

This tool helps you distinguish between spam and ham (legitimate) emails.

  • Spam emails are unsolicited messages sent in bulk, often for marketing purposes. They can be annoying and, in some cases, might contain malicious links or attachments.

  • Ham emails, on the other hand, are regular, non-spam emails that you want to receive.

  • Falling for a spam email can lead to various consequences, such as malware infection, personal data theft, and financial loss.

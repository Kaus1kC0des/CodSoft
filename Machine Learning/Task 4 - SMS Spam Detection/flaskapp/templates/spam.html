This E-Mail is SPAM!!

Spam messages are unsolicited, usually commercial messages sent to a large number of people. They are often sent by a bot or a compromised computer and can contain links to malicious websites or attachments with malware.

According to recent statistics, approximately 14.5 billion spam emails are sent every day globally, making up nearly 45% of all emails sent. These spam emails result in about $20.5 billion loss per year due to decreased productivity and technical expenses.
